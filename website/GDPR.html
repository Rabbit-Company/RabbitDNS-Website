General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

At Rabbit DNS, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Data Controller
The data controller for Rabbit DNS is Rabbit Company LLC, 1309 Coffeen Avenue STE 1200, Sheridan 82801, Wyoming USA, info@rabbit-company.com. We are responsible for ensuring that Rabbit DNS complies with the GDPR.

2. Legal Basis for Processing
Rabbit DNS does not process any personal data, and therefore does not require a legal basis for processing personal data under the GDPR.

3. Data Subjects' Rights
Data subjects have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • The right to be informed about the collection and use of their personal data. Rabbit DNS has informed data subjects through our Privacy Policy that we do not collect any personal data.
  • The right of access to their personal data. As Rabbit DNS does not collect any personal data, data subjects do not have any personal data to access.
  • The right to rectification of their personal data. As Rabbit DNS does not collect any personal data, data subjects do not have any personal data to rectify.
  • The right to erasure of their personal data. As Rabbit DNS does not collect any personal data, data subjects do not have any personal data to erase.
  • The right to restrict processing of their personal data. As Rabbit DNS does not collect any personal data, data subjects do not have any personal data to restrict processing of.
  • The right to data portability of their personal data. As Rabbit DNS does not collect any personal data, data subjects do not have any personal data to port.
  • The right to object to processing of their personal data. As Rabbit DNS does not collect any personal data, data subjects do not have any personal data to object to processing of.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Data subjects have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if they believe their rights under the GDPR have been violated.

4. Data Security
Rabbit DNS uses industry-standard security measures to protect user data. All DNS requests are encrypted with HTTPS to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of user data.

5. Data Breaches
In the unlikely event of a data breach, Rabbit DNS will take all necessary steps to comply with the GDPR's breach notification requirements.

6. Data Transfers
Rabbit DNS does not transfer any personal data outside of the European Economic Area (EEA). As we do not collect any personal data, we do not transfer any personal data at all.

7. Changes to GDPR
We may update this GDPR from time to time by posting a new version on Rabbit DNS. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are happy with any changes.

8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this GDPR, please contact us at info@rabbit-company.com.
